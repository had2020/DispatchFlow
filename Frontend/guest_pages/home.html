Right now, emergency coordination is slow and fragmented. Teams rely on outdated systems that delay response time, and in critical moments, that delay can cost lives.


DispatchFlow is a sort of live, Geosynchronous team organisation that works on a fast and safe architecture.


Benefits of using DispatchFlow

  • See your team live on a map, updated 100 times faster. The difference is Lightspeed, 10 minutes vs 10 seconds.
  • Easy-to-use team feature, allowing easy creation of teams and joining them.
  • Team chat allows quick communication, to coordinate, all on the same app for speed.
  • Open source, with readable documentation, to the non-technical on how to set up the project. Docs , and Github
  • No AI/LLM-generated code, all human-written for reliance.
  • Cheap and easy to setup from documentation, only requiring a 64-bit computer, like a Raspberry Pi Zero, which costs about $5, and a router to open a port on to get through NAT.
